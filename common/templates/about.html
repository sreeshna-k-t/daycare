ABOUT US

In our calicut university Daycare, we provide exceptional care and early education for children ages from 1 to 5. We are dedicated to creating a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can learn, grow, and thrive. Our experienced and passionate team of caregivers is committed to fostering the development of each child, supporting their social, emotional, cognitive, and physical growth. We believe in the power of play-based learning and offer a balanced curriculum that encourages exploration, creativity, and critical thinking. We invite you to visit calicut university Daycare and see firsthand how we create a nurturing and enriching environment for your child. Our friendly staff is available to answer any questions you may have and provide you with a tour of our facilities. Join us at calicut university Daycare, where we lay the foundation for a lifetime of learning and success. Contact us today to learn more and enroll your child in our program.
